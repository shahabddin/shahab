Workshops/Trainings

Solid Works with 3D Printing

Date: 22nd December 2022

Participated in a 2-days hands-on workshop on Solid Works with 3D Printing at the College of Electrical and Mechanical Engineering (EME), National University of Sciences and Technology (NUST), Islamabad. The workshop was aimed at teaching the basics of Solid Works and 3D Printing to the participants.

UR-5 Collaborative Robot

Date: 11th December 2022

Participated in a 2-day intensive hands-on workshop on the UR-5 Collaborative Robot at the College of Electrical and Mechanical Engineering (EME), National University of Sciences and Technology (NUST), Islamabad. The workshop focused on programming and operating the UR-5 robot for various industrial applications.

PCB Design & Fabrication

Date: 24th November 2022

Participated in a 2-days hands-on workshop on PCB Design & Fabrication at the College of Electrical and Mechanical Engineering (EME), National University of Sciences and Technology (NUST), Islamabad. The workshop covered the entire process from designing to fabricating PCBs, providing hands-on experience with industry-standard tools and techniques.

Aerial Spraying Drones

Date: 16th October 2022

Organised and managed a 2-days workshop on Aerial Spraying Drones at the College of Aeronautical Engineering (CAE), National University of Sciences and Technology (NUST), Islamabad. The workshop aimed at introducing the participants to the operation and applications of aerial spraying drones in agriculture.
