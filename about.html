Biography

I am a dedicated researcher with expertise in Computer Vision, IoT, and Machine Learning, specializing in deep learning, image processing, and embedded systems. I hold a Bachelor of Science degree in Electrical Engineering from the University of Engineering and Technology Peshawar, Pakistan where I developed a strong foundation in communication systems. My passion for research and innovation has led me to work on cutting-edge projects that integrate AI and automation for real-world applications. Currently, I am serving as a Research Assistant at the College of Aeronautical Engineering (CAE), National University of Sciences & Technology (NUST), Islamabad, Pakistan, where I am leading the development of a portable scanning system for the detection of rivet-related defects in aircraft structures using lock-in thermography.

Previously, I worked as a Research Assistant at the National Center of Robotics & Automation (NCRA), National University of Sciences & Technology (NUST), Islamabad, contributing to the design and implementation of computer vision and IoT-based solutions. My notable works include a Skeletal Keypoint-Based Transformer Model for Human Action Recognition in Aerial Videos, which utilizes deep learning techniques for activity recognition. Beyond research, I have led and contributed to multiple projects, such as an intrusion detection system using YOLO and DeepSORT, a medical otoscope for ear canal diagnosis, and the development of a portable scanning system for defective rivets detection based on Lock-in Thermography using Raspberry Pi, Jetson Nano, Arduino, and ESP32 etc. My expertise in AI and deep learning is reflected in my research publications in renowned journals such as IEEE Access and Mechanical Systems and Signal Processing. My technical proficiency spans Programming, IoT and Embedded Systems, with extensive experience in AI model optimization, data wrangling and cleaning. My commitment to continuous learning is evident through multiple certifications in Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) equipping me with advanced skills in CNN architectures, NLP models, and AI-driven image processing. My contributions have earned me recognition, including the Prime Minister Laptop Scheme Award for academic excellence, and I am a registered engineer with the Pakistan Engineering Council (PEC). In addition to my research and technical endeavors, I actively engage in professional development through hands-on workshops and training in areas like PCB design, 3D printing, collaborative robotics, and aerial drone technology. With a research-driven mindset and strong technical acumen, I am deeply committed to advancing AI, automation, and intelligent systems, aiming to develop innovative solutions that push the boundaries of technology and its real-world applications.

I am actively looking for research collaboration as well as scholarship opportunities for higher studies.
