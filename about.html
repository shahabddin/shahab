Biography

I am a dedicated researcher with almost 3 years of experience in Image Processing, Computer Vision, Deep Learning and IoT. I hold a Bachelor of Science degree in Electrical Engineering from the University of Engineering and Technology Peshawar, Pakistan. My passion for research and innovation has led me to work on cutting-edge projects that integrate AI and automation for real-world applications. Currently, I am serving as a Research Assistant at the College of Aeronautical Engineering (CAE), National University of Sciences & Technology (NUST), Islamabad, Pakistan, where I am leading the development of a portable scanning system for the detection of rivet-related defects in aircraft structures using lock-in thermography.

Previously, I worked as a Research Assistant at the National Center of Robotics & Automation (NCRA), National University of Sciences & Technology (NUST), Islamabad, Pakistan, contributing to the design and implementation of computer vision and IoT-based solutions. My notable works include a Skeletal Keypoint-Based Transformer Model for Human Action Recognition in Aerial Videos, which utilizes deep learning techniques for activity recognition. Also, I have led and contributed to multiple projects, such as an intrusion detection system using YOLO and DeepSORT, early forest fire detection and a medical otoscope for ear canal diagnosis. My expertise in Image Processing and AI is reflected in my research publications in renowned journals such as IEEE Access and another in Mechanical Systems and Signal Processing. My technical proficiency spans Programming (Python, MATLAB, Linux), IoT (Arduino, ESP32) and Embedded Systems (Raspberry Pi, Jetson Nano). My commitment to continuous learning is evident through multiple certifications in Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) equipping me with advanced skills in CNN architectures, NLP models, and AI-driven image processing. With a research-driven mindset and strong technical acumen, I am deeply committed to advancing AI, automation, and intelligent systems, aiming to develop innovative solutions that push the boundaries of technology and its real-world applications.

I am actively looking for research collaboration as well as scholarship opportunities for higher studies.
